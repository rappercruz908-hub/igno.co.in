Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about IGNO and our AI solutions

What are agentic AI systems?

+

Agentic AI systems are intelligent software agents that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve specific goals autonomously. Unlike traditional automation that follows predefined rules, agentic systems can reason through complex scenarios, adapt to changing conditions, and learn from experience. They work more like a smart assistant that understands context and can figure out how to accomplish tasks even when faced with unexpected situations.

How is IGNO different from other automation tools?

+

While many automation tools require you to manually map out every step of a process, IGNO's agentic AI can understand high-level goals and figure out how to achieve them. We focus on building systems that can handle exceptions, adapt to changes, and make intelligent decisions—not just follow scripts. Our solutions integrate deeply with your existing systems and learn from how your business actually operates, rather than forcing you to change your workflows to fit a rigid automation framework.

What types of businesses can benefit from IGNO?

+

We work with growing businesses and mid-market companies across various industries—from e-commerce and SaaS to professional services and logistics. If you're dealing with repetitive workflows, managing high volumes of data, or spending too much time on tasks that could be automated, IGNO can help. We're particularly effective for companies that have outgrown basic automation tools but aren't yet ready for enterprise-level complexity and cost.

How long does implementation take?

+

It depends on the scope and complexity of your project. A focused automation project might take 2-4 weeks from kickoff to deployment, while more comprehensive implementations typically take 6-12 weeks. We follow an iterative approach, so you'll see results early and often rather than waiting months for a big reveal. We prioritize getting value into your hands quickly and then expanding based on what works best.

How do you handle data security?

+

Security is fundamental to everything we build. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest, implement strict access controls, and follow security best practices throughout development. We can work within your existing security framework and are happy to sign NDAs and data processing agreements. For businesses with specific compliance requirements, we can deploy solutions in your own infrastructure or private cloud environment.

What kind of ROI should we expect?

+

ROI varies based on your specific use case, but our clients typically see significant time savings (40-60% reduction in manual work) and cost reductions within the first few months. The benefits compound over time as the system learns and optimizes. We work with you upfront to identify clear success metrics and track progress toward those goals. Most clients find that the automation pays for itself within 6-12 months through a combination of time savings, error reduction, and improved efficiency.
